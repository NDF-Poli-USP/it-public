# 📢 Usage Instructions – Intelbras Alarm System | NDF

## 🔒 Password Updates

The Intelbras Alarm System password is updated **every semester** as part of the security policies of the Fluids & Dynamics Research Group (NDF).

**Important:**  
The password may be changed at any time if required by the IT team, **without prior individual notice**.  
In such cases, the updated password will be communicated via the official NDF email group.

To request access, please contact the **NDF-IT team**.

---

## 🛠️ System Operation

Follow the procedure below to access **Research Rooms 1, 2, and 3, the Meeting Room, and the Classroom** **without triggering the alarm**:

### ✅ Step-by-Step

1. Go to the **Intelbras Alarm System control panel**.  
2. Verify that the panel is powered on and operational.  
3. Enter the current password (available via institutional email or upon request to NDF-IT).  

---

## ⏰ Alarm Arming Policy

The NDF alarm system must be **manually armed by the last person leaving the facility**.

As a safety fallback, the system is **automatically armed every day at 00:30** if it has not been armed manually.

---

## ⚠️ Access Restrictions

- It is **not permitted to remain inside the building after 23:00 without prior authorization**.
- Any presence after this time must be **previously approved**.
- Unauthorized access or presence may trigger the alarm and lead to security incidents.

---

## 🔔 Operational Notes

- Ensure the alarm is properly armed whenever leaving the NDF.
- If authorized to stay after 23:00, you must:
  - Coordinate in advance with the **NDF-IT team**
  - Properly operate the alarm system (disarm and re-arm as required)
- Failure to follow these procedures may result in unintended alarm activation.

---

## ⚠️ Security Guidelines

- The password is for **exclusive use by authorized NDF researchers**.
- It must only be used for access to:
  - Research Rooms 1, 2, and 3  
  - Meeting Room  
  - Classroom
- Password sharing is strictly prohibited.
- Misuse may result in security incidents and administrative actions.

For support or incident reporting, contact the **NDF-IT team**.
